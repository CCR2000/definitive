Let's create a fancy podcast

Podcast with garageband

A podcast is an audio recording that you can listen to whenever you want. It is like a radio programme but digital.

Our podcast will be called 'Describe your costume!' and each of you will record your own episode in English.

We are going to record our podcast with GarageBand on the tablet.

1.   Open GarageBand on your tablet.

2.   Select 'Audio Recorder'.

3.   Press the red record button.

4.  Read your script out loud and clearly.

5.  Stop the recording and listen to it.

6. If you are happy with it, save it.

Esta actividad también será evaluada.
