My costume card

Canva Costume card

We are going to create our digital costume card with Canva!

 Sigue estos pasos: / Follow these steps:

1.   Abre Canva for Education en tu tablet. / Open Canva for Education on your tablet.

2.   Abre el enlace a la plantilla de Canva. / Open the template link: https://canva.link/ecw0ct21ak9iyew

3.   Elige una imagen para tu personaje disfrazado. / Choose an image for your costume character.

4.   Escribe frases en inglés para describirlo: 'My character is wearing...' / Write a sentence English and describe it.

5.   Guarda tu tarjeta y compártela en Teams. / Save your card and share it in Teams.

Watch the video to help with your descriptions!

Antes de publicar nuestra tarjeta, vamos a pensar juntos...

Before we publish our card, let's think together...

 

Preguntas para reflexionar: / Questions to think about:

•     ¿Que informacion hemos puesto en nuestra tarjeta? / What information have we put on our card?

•     ¿Es seguro poner nuestra foto real en internet? / Is it safe to put our real photo on the internet?

•     ¿Que diferencia hay entre un disfraz y nuestra identidad real? / What is the difference between a costume and our real identity?

 

Remember: online we only use our first name. We never share surnames, real photos or addresses.

True or false

Pregunta 1

We always share our personal pictures on Internet so everybody knows who we are :) 

Retroalimentación

Falso
