Ask and guess!

Affirmative (+)

Question (?)

Negative (-)

He is wearing a wig.

She is wearing glasses.

They are wearing costumes.

Is he wearing a wig?

Is she wearing glasses?

What is he wearing?

He is not wearing a wig.

She isn't wearing glasses.

They aren't wearing costumes.
